ARC Initiative mission
We approach development initiatives from a perspective that puts people and their capabilities first.


_________________________________________________________________

With our efforts we aim to:

  • Expand and enhance people's choices
  • Create or ensure the opportunity to define and practice those choices within the three pillars of sustainability

_________________________________________________________________

We build our organization around our core values:

Community participation

  • To promote capacity building and empowerment
  • To leverage and build on existing knowledge
  • To preserve and strengthen community values and institutions

Collaborations and partnerships with multiple actors and stakeholders

  • To forge and foster links, network and learn from past initiatives
  • To use existing and emerging knowledge, research and tools from partnerships between academia and development organizations
  • To support long-term sustainability through effective cooperation

Integrated and holistic approaches

  • That identify all connected systems for improved understanding of system dynamics
  • To ensure that all related systems are taken into considerations when implementing strategies
